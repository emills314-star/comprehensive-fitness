# App Store Preparation Notes

## App Identity

- App name: Comprehensive Fitness
- Bundle ID: com.emills.comprehensivefitness
- Category: Health & Fitness
- Price: Free

## Short Description

Log strength workouts, track sets and RPE, time rests, and get transparent progression guidance.

## Review Notes

Comprehensive Fitness is a local-first workout tracker. It does not require an account. Workout data is stored on device. The app includes export/import controls for user-managed backups.

Progression guidance is informational and based on logged workout history, completion, load, RPE, travel status, and recent trend rules. It is not medical advice.
