Privacy Policy

Comprehensive Fitness is a local-first workout tracker. The app stores workout sessions, exercise names, sets, reps, weights, RPE, notes, and settings on your device.

Data Collection

The app does not require an account. Workout data is stored locally in IndexedDB. Rest notifications and workout cloud copy are separate optional controls. Enabling notifications sends only the installation-scoped push subscription and timer details needed to deliver requested alerts; it does not enable workout upload. Workout copies are uploaded only after you explicitly enable Optional workout cloud copy in Settings.

Third Parties

The app does not include advertising or analytics trackers. Optional notifications use Vercel Functions, Upstash QStash, Upstash Redis, and the browser push service. Push endpoints and encryption keys are used only to deliver requested alerts.

Your Control

You can export your data, import a backup, or clear local data from the Settings screen. Optional server workout copies expire after at most 90 days. Turning cloud copy off stops and clears pending uploads and requests deletion of retained copies. Disabling notifications revokes the push subscription independently. Clearing local data revokes the installation credential and deletes retained workout copies before removing the local credential; the app waits for a network connection rather than orphaning server data.

Training Guidance

Progression recommendations are informational fitness guidance and are not medical advice. Use your own judgment and consult a qualified professional before making health or training decisions.
